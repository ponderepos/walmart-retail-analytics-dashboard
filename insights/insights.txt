# Key Business Insights

This document highlights the major insights derived from the Walmart Sales and Inventory Analytics Dashboard.

---

# 1. Revenue Performance

Total sales revenue reached approximately **1.10 billion**, indicating strong overall business performance.

The gross profit of **104.47 million** shows a stable profit margin across product categories.

---

# 2. Product Revenue Leaders

The dashboard highlights several top-performing products:

* iPhone
* Laptop i7
* Laptop i5
* Android Phones

These products generate the highest revenue and represent key drivers of business growth.

---

# 3. Regional Sales Distribution

Sales performance is distributed across four major regions:

* East
* West
* North
* South

All regions contribute relatively balanced sales volumes, indicating a well-distributed market presence.

---

# 4. Year-over-Year Revenue Growth

Revenue shows positive growth from **2023 to 2025**, with an increase of approximately **7.63% in 2024** and **5.09% in 2025**.

However, a significant decline appears in **2026**, indicating potential demand fluctuations or inventory changes.

---

# 5. Category Contribution

Electronics contributes the highest share of total revenue compared to:

* Home & Kitchen
* Fashion
* Grocery

This indicates strong consumer demand for electronic products.

---

# 6. Inventory Performance

The inventory dashboard shows:

* Opening Inventory: 926K
* Units Purchased: 178K
* Units Sold: 141K
* Closing Inventory: 963K

This indicates a stable inventory supply supporting ongoing sales demand.

---

# 7. High Volume Cities

The cities with the highest sales volume include:

* Kolkata
* Mumbai
* Delhi
* Hyderabad

These locations represent key markets for retail sales growth.

---

# Conclusion

The Walmart Sales and Inventory Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of retail business performance.

By combining sales and inventory insights, the dashboard enables stakeholders to make **better inventory planning decisions, identify revenue drivers, and monitor regional sales trends effectively**.
