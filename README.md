# Walmart Sales & Inventory Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a **Walmart Sales and Inventory Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze retail sales performance, product trends, and inventory efficiency across multiple regions.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor **sales growth, product performance, inventory levels, and regional distribution**, helping decision-makers optimize inventory management and improve business performance.

The project demonstrates skills in **data modeling, KPI design, business analytics, and dashboard storytelling**.

---

# Dashboard Modules

The dashboard consists of two main analytical views:

## 1. Sales Performance Dashboard

The Sales Dashboard provides insights into overall business performance and product revenue.

Key KPIs displayed:

* Total Revenue: **1.10B**
* Gross Margin: **9.47%**
* Cost of Goods Sold: **998.61M**
* Gross Profit: **104.47M**
* Quantity Sold: **82K**

Main analytical visuals include:

* Sales trend analysis by region
* Top products by revenue
* Profit contribution by city
* Year-over-year revenue growth
* Units sold by region

This view helps management identify **high-performing products, revenue drivers, and regional sales performance**.

---

## 2. Inventory Performance Dashboard

The Inventory Dashboard provides insights into stock levels, product demand, and supply efficiency.

Key KPIs displayed:

* Opening Inventory: **926K**
* Units Purchased: **178K**
* Units Sold: **141K**
* Closing Inventory: **963K**
* Inventory Turnover: **1.06K**

Main analytical visuals include:

* Top products by volume
* Inventory distribution by region
* Revenue contribution by category
* Top cities by product volume
* Inventory trend and YoY growth

This dashboard helps organizations monitor **inventory movement, product demand, and regional stock distribution**.
